# Novelty Decision

Decision: not enough for submission.

The idea remains plausible: physical resistance probes can reveal latent fixtures. The evidence confirms that fixture probes are useful, because the prototype system-ID baseline performs well. However, the proposed structured latent-fixture inference mechanism does not outperform that simpler baseline.

The novelty boundary collapses in the current implementation because the method's handcrafted fixture semantics are less reliable than prototype matching over probe traces.
